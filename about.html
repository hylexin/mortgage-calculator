About MortgageCalc

MortgageCalc is a simple, fast mortgage calculator built to help home buyers estimate monthly payments without creating an account. The calculator includes principal and interest, property taxes, homeowners insurance, PMI, HOA fees, extra payments, biweekly payment estimates, payoff date, and a mortgage amortization schedule.

The goal is to make mortgage math easier to understand. The page explains the major inputs and shows how payments change over time, so visitors can compare scenarios before speaking with a lender or real estate professional.

MortgageCalc is not a lender, broker, real estate company, or credit decision platform. It does not provide loan offers, pre-approvals, APR disclosures, or personalized financial advice.

This site is educational only. It does not provide financial, tax, legal, or lending advice.
